The show

The "LadyGaga LoveGame" tribute show is an outstanding Tribute that pays honor to the wild Queen of Pop, Lady Gaga. 

The show is a visually stunning and dramatic theatrical performance that recreates all of the drama, glitz and glamour of Lady Gaga's most memorable performances with a modern, powerful and provocative twist. 

It’s fast-paced, visually exhilarating and is unbeaten entertainment value for all ages, and venues. 


Show Formats: Solo, with dancers, with live band or with live band AND dancers 

Set Lengths: Show can be anything from 20 minutes to a full on 1 and a half hour spectacular!

Sara Luh alias Lady Gaga 

Sara Luh, an Essex based pro singer/songwriter of 10 years, with a music degree, has a diverse musical background, from singing in Funky/Pop bands, through to jazz big bands! 
Just like Gaga, she loves to get her audience rocking with evocative and dynamic performances of powerful hits such as Bad Romance and Judas, as well as reaching out to the crowd with the singer-songwriter majestic anthems of Applause and Born this way. 
Sara Luh has injected her years of musicianship and stage craft, from having worked in major hotel chains and on- board ships, into her Gaga show, along with her natural creativity. 
Sara Luh is the Lady as soon as she puts the wig and glasses on with totally outrageous behaviour, amazing costume changes and stunning 'LIVE' vocals she really could be mistaken for the world class performer and Grammy winner... Lady Gaga herself!
